# Campaign Deactivation

Deactivation stops fundraising while keeping the page up.

## When to deactivate
- You reached your goal early
- You want to pause donations
- You discovered an issue and need to regroup

## What happens after deactivation
- Donations are disabled
- Campaign status shows as ended
- Updates remain readable
