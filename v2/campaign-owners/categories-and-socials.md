# Categories & socials

Categories help discovery and context. Social links help trust.

## Categories
You can select multiple.
Typical options include:
- Art & Culture
- Community
- Education
- Environment & Energy
- Health & Wellness
- NGO / Nonprofits
- Technology
- Others

## Social links
You can add up to 5.

Best practice:
- Link the “primary proof” first (website / repo)
- Then add your community hubs (X, Discord, Telegram, etc.)
