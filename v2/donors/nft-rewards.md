# NFT Rewards & Donor Levels

CrowdWalrus includes a participation layer: **NFT badge rewards**.

## What you earn
When you donate, the system can mint an NFT badge to your wallet.

Badges are:
- visible on your profile
- a fun proof of participation

## How levels are earned
Levels typically depend on:
- your total contributions (USD value)
- number of donations

Thresholds may evolve over time.

## Where badges show up
- your CrowdWalrus profile
- your wallet (as NFTs)
