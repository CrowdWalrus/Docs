# Campaign Writing Toolkit

A solid campaign is 80% clarity and 20% vibes.

## The winning structure
1. **The hook** (what’s at stake, in one paragraph)
2. **The problem** (why this matters)
3. **The plan** (what you’ll do next)
4. **The budget** (where funds go)
5. **The proof** (links, track record, milestones)
6. **The ask** (what supporters should do now)

## Template: short pitch (280 chars)
> We’re building **[thing]** for **[who]** on Sui. We’ve shipped **[proof]** and need **$[goal]** to deliver **[milestone]** by **[date]**. Support us, share it, and help us ship.

## Template: full story
**What we’re building**
- …

**Why now**
- …

**Milestones**
- Milestone 1 (date): …
- Milestone 2 (date): …

**Budget**
- $X — …
- $Y — …

**Proof**
- Repo: …
- Demo: …
- Prior work: …

**Call to action**
- Donate, share, and follow updates.

## Proof & trust checklist
- [ ] Team identities (names or on-chain profiles)
- [ ] Links to code or prior work
- [ ] A realistic budget
- [ ] Milestones + timeline
- [ ] Clear recipient wallet explanation
