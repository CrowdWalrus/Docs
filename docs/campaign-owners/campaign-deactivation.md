# Campaign Deactivation

Deactivation stops fundraising while keeping the page up.

## When to deactivate
- You delivered the milestones, impact, rewards
- You want to pause donations or contributions
- You discovered an issue and need to regroup

## What happens after deactivation

- Campaign status shows as ended
- Donations are disabled 
- Updates remain readable

## Can I reactivate?
- Reactivation are always possible
- Campaigns will get back to normal mode, like active, funding ...
- Campaign owner can post updates again.
