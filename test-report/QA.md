Additionally, some potential testers were not yet onboarded into the **Sui ecosystem** and first needed to install a compatible wallet and complete initial setup before they could begin testing.

The QA phase also kicked off **close to the Christmas and year-end holiday period**, which naturally reduced user availability and responsiveness and had a measurable impact on live user testing velocity.
